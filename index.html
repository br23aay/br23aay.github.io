Graceful Under Pressure
First systematic tactile-sensor failure robustness study for 24-DoF in-hand RL policies. Ablates random dropout, stuck-at faults, calibration drift, and additive noise on trained Shadow Hand PPO. Proposes a dropout-augmentation training curriculum. Directly addresses the sim-to-real gap for Shadow Hand deployment.
→ Testing now · MuJoCo sensor ablation · Target: IEEE RA-L / Frontiers Robotics & AI

DexHackBench
First reward-hacking benchmark and trace-based detector for dexterous in-hand manipulation. Instruments phase-based reward shaping with KL divergence and correlation-decay detectors on the Shadow Hand. Existing MuJoCo reward-hacking literature (arXiv:2507.05619) covers locomotion only — not high-DoF hands. Novel gap confirmed.

TraceMonitor
CPU-resident Small Language Model runtime safety monitor for reward hacking and unsafe behaviour in dexterous RL policies. Converts proprioceptive, tactile, and reward traces into textual summaries — prompts Phi-3 Mini (Ollama, CPU) to classify each window as nominal vs. hacking with a natural-language explanation. First SLM runtime monitor for in-hand manipulation.
