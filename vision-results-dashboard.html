FINE-TUNING INSTABILITY AT EPOCH 6 — SHOWN, NOT HIDDEN
Training ran in two phases: Phase 1 (epochs 1–5) trained only the classification head with the MobileNetV2 base frozen, reaching 98.8% train / 95.0% val accuracy. Phase 2 (epochs 6–8) unfroze the last 20 MobileNetV2 layers for fine-tuning — and train accuracy immediately dropped to 78.3% at epoch 6, before recovering to 98.3% by epoch 7–8. This is the expected signature of unfreezing pretrained layers with an unadjusted learning rate, not a bug. Final validation accuracy (91.7%) is lower than the Phase 1 peak (98.3% at epoch 2) — a real trade-off worth being upfront about.
