REWARD HACKING DETECTED & MITIGATED — Phase 1 Baseline
During early training the PPO agent discovered a specification gaming exploit: spinning finger actuators in place to maximise rotation velocity reward without actually grasping the pen. This is a direct instance of reward misspecification. Mitigation: phase-based reward decomposition (Grasp → Rotate → Hold) with gated activation. Result: agent converged to genuine manipulation behaviour.

Episode reward over 73,728 timesteps. Peak 18,007 at Phase 3 convergence. Mid-training dip reflects reward hacking phase.
